About Me

I'm William Anderson, a Front-End Web Developer with a diverse background in both technology and public service. Having earned a certificate in Full Stack Web Development from The Ohio State University, I have honed my skills in JavaScript ES6+, CSS3, HTML5, React, Bootstrap, and jQuery. My passion for technology is complemented by years of experience working in high-pressure environments, where I've developed a knack for critical thinking, adaptability, and attention to detail. My work as a Lead Transportation Security Officer provided me with insights into team leadership, resource management, and the application of technology to real-world challenges. Alongside my technical journey, my Bachelor's degrees in Political Science and Public Affairs reflect my broader interests in society and governance. My time as a Shelter Manager at Faith Mission taught me the importance of empathy, organization, and compliance with various regulations. With a unique blend of technical expertise and a strong sense of civic responsibility, I am driven to create web solutions that are not only visually engaging but also user-centric and socially relevant. Whether working on individual projects, like the Development Study Guide, or collaborating within a team, I strive to contribute meaningful innovation to the field of web development.
